Legends of Indian Cricket

Virender Sehwag

Full name :Virender Sehwag

Born :October 20, 1978, Delhi

Current age :38 years 247 days

Major teams :India,Asia XI, Delhi, Delhi Daredevils, ICC World XI, India Blue, Kings XI Punjab, Leicestershire, Marylebone Cricket Club, Raj Cricket Ass. President's XI

Playing role : Top-order batsman

Batting style : Right-hand bat

Bowling style : Right-arm offbreak

Sachin R. Tendulkar

Full name :Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar

Born :April 24, 1973, Bombay (now Mumbai), Maharashtra

Current age : 44 years 62 days

Major teams :India,Asia XI, Mumbai, Mumbai Indians, Yorkshire

Playing role : Top-order batsman

Batting style : Right-hand bat

Bowling style : Right-arm offbreak, Legbreak googly

In a nutshell : Perhaps the most complete batsman and the most worshipped cricketer in the world, Tendulkar holds just about every batting record worth owning in the game, including those for most runs and hundreds in Tests and ODIs, and most international runs.
